FindUSCHousingüè† (Spring 2023)

I collaborated with six classmates in CSCI 201 to build a dynamic, full stack website called "FindUSCHousing." Our goal was to create a platform where current USC students can sublease their units to other students, or search for housing being subleased by other students. While we never officially released our product for public use, it was a great learning experience. We used html, css, bootstrap, and cookies for the front-end. For the back-end, we used Java servlets and an SQL database. Below, are some pictures of the various pages my team built. I worked on implementing the front-end and back-end of the profile page, which displays the user's personal information like their name, email address, and any rentals they have saved or posted on the site.
